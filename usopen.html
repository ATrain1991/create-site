Accommodations

Hotel & Robot Shipping

Host Hotels

CREATE partners with host hotels in the Council Bluffs / Omaha area that offer robot shipping options, making it easy for out-of-town teams to attend without the hassle of transporting robots on a plane.

For reservations and a full list of host hotels, contact us at Support@Create-Found.org or call (402) 614-2210.
