Our Purpose

Built Around a Single Mission

To inspire and prepare the youth of today to become the engineers and scientists of tomorrow — through competitive robotics that is compelling, accessible, and safe.

Our competitions are sports-like head-to-head challenges that stretch students' technical knowledge while sharpening teamwork, communication, and leadership skills.

About Us

A Nonprofit Built on Volunteers

The CREATE Foundation is a 501(c)(3) non-profit headquartered in Omaha, NE. Since 2008 we've supported students, teachers, and mentors in advancing STEM education through world-class robotics competition.

We also host international cultural exchange camps that blend language, technical skills, and cross-cultural understanding — inviting international teams to join U.S. teams in a unique learning experience.

Governed by an all-volunteer Board of Directors and Advisory Board located throughout the United States.
